**What all things to look after while choosing database**

|||
|---|---|
|**Storage Layer**|- Base storage engine - Used Trees, Leaf nodes optimization or any other mechanism<br>    <br>- Indexes storage both local and global<br>    <br>- Storage replication<br>    <br>- Dead row removal mechanism<br>    <br>- Backup<br>    <br>- Data locality in the cluster<br>    <br>- Conflict resolution during stale updates|
|**Replication**|- How data is replicated and latencies<br>    <br>- Master and replica locality<br>    <br>- In-Sync replication or Quorum capabilities<br>    <br>- Cross data center replication techniques<br>    <br>- CDC both in cross datacenter and primary|
|**Transaction**|- Types of isolation level supported<br>    <br>- How distributed transaction managed and throughput<br>    <br>- Transaction conflict resolution<br>    <br>- Transaction orchestration both storage<br>    <br>- How transaction are managed|
|**Query**|- Query engine used and dialect<br>    <br>-  Distributed select and penalties<br>    <br>-  Clock synchronisation|
|**Sharding**|- Re-sharding/Partition penalties by adding node/manual re-sharding<br>    <br>- Failover latencies.<br>    <br>- Failover mechanism used|
|**Upgrades**|- Backward compatibility<br>    <br>-  Roll over upgrades . Here need to check about replication backward support|
|**Observability**|- metrics|
|**Connection Management**|- connection pooling<br>    <br>- Multiplexing<br>    <br>- Connection penalties - Forking process or some other mechanism|
